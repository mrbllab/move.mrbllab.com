MOVE

The Move2Earn App of Telegram

Move is a Pokémon GO style Telegram mini app that rewards users with $move tokens for every kilometer they walk or run, using step and location tracking. Currently in development, Move is set to launch in September.

Game Mechanics

Interactive Map with Player Rewards

Move starts with a map and a character. As users walk and explore their surroundings, various reward items, like treasure chests, will appear on the map, offering the chance to walk up and collect the rewards.

Level-Up System

The more a user engages with the app - by walking and completing tasks - the more $move tokens they earn and the higher level they become. As their level increases, so does their earning rate per kilometer walked.

Leagues & Leaderboards

Each user is placed into a weekly league based on the amount of $move they've earned. At the end of the week, top players advance to a higher league, while the bottom players move down. Each league determines the bonus earned per kilometer walked.

User Profiles

Players can follow their friends and track their progress through user profiles. Each profile displays earnings and achievement stats, along with a unique and customizable avatar.

Daily Tasks and Achievements

To keep users engaged, a variety of daily tasks and achievements offer rewards that boost both $move earnings and the $move/km earning rate.

Marketplace

Users can buy and sell special clothes and items for their characters using TON NFTs, encouraging ongoing engagement and continued earning of $move tokens even after the TGE.
